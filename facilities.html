PANIMALAR INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY (PIT)

FACILITIES IN OUR COLLEGE

LIBRARY

* A Separate fully air conditioned library with capacity of nearly 80 seats has exclusively been devoted to first year and the Department of Information and Technology. It has an excellent reading hall that has reference books, hand books, dictionaries and encyclopedias. Every Student and staff can become a member of this Library. Each student is given 4 books at a time and has been allocated library exclusive hours in a week. Library is kept open from 7:30 A.M to 8:00 P.M. (Week days) and 8:00 A.M to 3:00 P.M. (Saturdays). The PIT campus spreads over 600 acres in a spacious ,noise free , claen & green zone and serene atmosphere ideally suited for technical education. The infrastructure and facilities available on campus are the very best. It is a wholly self-contained campus contain everything that students on campus would ever require

PIT HOSTEL

*The campus life at Panimalar is wholesome, easy and fun filled. It provides the students a judicious mix of education and enjoyment. The Panimalar campus offers to both the day scholars and those who stay in hostel a complete suite of facilities that are very self sufficient. Thus the student has absolutely no need to step out of the college premises for anything. It goes that extra mile to make the life of students staying at hostels that much more comfortable and pleasant, thereby making it a second home. *The college provides excellent and separate hostel facilities for both boys and girls. The students are provided spacious rooms with attached bath. Guest rooms are also made available in both the boys and girls hostels. Subscribing to the fact that students need to have fresh air combined with physical sport it offers spacious and large playcourts/playgroud for volleyball, basketball, kabadi ball badminton and other outdoor games.

MESS

*The Institute has a well ventilated, pest free and magnificent mess with marble seating arrangements. Separate rows for vegetarians and non-vegetarians. Separate seating arrangements for boys and girls. Separate rows for the students and staff members. A home away from home ambience.

*Well lit and spotlessly maintained mess. Gentle and affectionate servers serving nutritious, delicious and wholesome food. Glittering and clean stainless steel utensils are used The hot, fresh and sumptuous food at stipulated time intervals. Festive look. Unlimited tiffen, meals & Dinner.Every effort has been made to provide congenial ambience to the students and staff. Many students in and around Tamil Nadu stay in the Institute's hostel and study here. So it is well and good if homely atmosphere is provided to them.The mess facility is also one such.S
